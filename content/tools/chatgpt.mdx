## Overview

ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI assistant that works well for idea generation, drafts, and quick technical guidance. It is especially useful when you need to move from a blank page to a first version quickly.

## Best For

- Students building first drafts for assignments and presentations
- Developers writing documentation, scripts, and debugging notes
- Teams that need rapid brainstorming and content iteration

## Strengths

- Fast, high-quality drafting across many domains
- Strong conversational context handling for iterative refinement
- Useful for both technical and non-technical workflows

## Limitations

- Can still hallucinate facts in niche domains
- Needs explicit prompts for reliable structure and tone
- Advanced usage often benefits from paid tier limits

## Verdict

If you need one assistant for everyday ideation and execution, this is one of the strongest defaults.
