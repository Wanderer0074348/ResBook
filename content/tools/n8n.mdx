## Overview

n8n helps teams automate repetitive processes by connecting services, adding conditions, and running AI-assisted actions in repeatable pipelines.

## Best For

- Internal ops automation and workflow orchestration
- Teams combining APIs, databases, and AI calls
- Rapid proof-of-concept agent pipelines before custom code

## Strengths

- Flexible visual builder with good extensibility
- Effective for integrating many tools without deep custom backend work
- Useful observability and step-level debugging

## Limitations

- Complex workflows still require engineering discipline
- Poorly designed flows can become hard to maintain
- Enterprise-scale governance needs explicit planning

## Verdict

A practical foundation for AI-powered automation when used with clear architecture.
