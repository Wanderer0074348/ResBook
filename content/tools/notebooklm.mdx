## Overview

NotebookLM is built for working with your own material, making it useful for study, research briefs, and document-heavy workflows.

## Best For

- Students preparing from notes and reading packs
- Researchers synthesizing multiple documents
- Teams creating concise internal knowledge digests

## Strengths

- Strong at source-grounded summaries
- Reduces context switching across many files
- Useful for extracting structured takeaways quickly

## Limitations

- Quality depends on source document quality and coverage
- Not a replacement for deep expert validation
- Less suited for full software engineering workflows

## Verdict

Highly practical for document-first learning and research synthesis workflows.
