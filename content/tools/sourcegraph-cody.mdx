## Overview

Cody is especially helpful in larger repositories where code understanding and cross-file context are more important than just inline autocomplete.

## Best For

- Navigating complex multi-module codebases
- Refactoring with broad context awareness
- Teams working in enterprise-scale repositories

## Strengths

- Strong repo-wide context and search integration
- Useful for onboarding into unfamiliar codebases
- Helpful for architecture-level comprehension tasks

## Limitations

- Value is lower on very small projects
- Requires careful prompt framing for precise changes
- Full benefit often depends on team adoption patterns

## Verdict

Excellent for medium-to-large codebases where context quality is critical.
