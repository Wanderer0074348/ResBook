## Overview

Perplexity is optimized for discovery and research, making it easy to collect references, compare viewpoints, and generate a concise first brief.

## Best For

- Fast market and topic research
- Students creating reference-backed notes
- Teams that need source-oriented answer trails

## Strengths

- Source-cited responses improve traceability
- Quick synthesis of broad topics and trends
- Strong utility for “start here” research passes

## Limitations

- Not ideal for deep coding/refactoring workflows
- Source quality still depends on query framing
- Follow-up prompts are required for nuanced depth

## Verdict

Excellent as a front-door research assistant before deeper analysis in another tool.
