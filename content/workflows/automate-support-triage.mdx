## Goal

Reduce manual support load by auto-classifying and routing incoming tickets with guardrails.

<WorkflowStep step={1} title="Design Triage Taxonomy">
Define categories, priority levels, and escalation conditions for your support queue.
</WorkflowStep>

<WorkflowStep step={2} title="Orchestrate Pipeline in n8n">
Use n8n to ingest tickets, call LLM classification steps, and route results to team channels.

<PromptBlock agent="USER">
Classify this support request into category, urgency, and recommended owner. Return strict JSON.
</PromptBlock>
</WorkflowStep>

<WorkflowStep step={3} title="Add Stateful Guardrails">
Use LangGraph for multi-step validation when confidence is low and force human review for risky categories.
</WorkflowStep>

<WorkflowStep step={4} title="Measure and Improve">
Track misroutes weekly and tune prompts/rules until triage accuracy reaches your target threshold.
</WorkflowStep>

## Expected Output

A repeatable triage pipeline that handles common tickets automatically and escalates sensitive cases safely.
