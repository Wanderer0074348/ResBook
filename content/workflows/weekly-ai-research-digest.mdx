## Goal

Produce a concise weekly digest of AI trends with source references and clear action points.

<WorkflowStep step={1} title="Collect Source Material">
Use Perplexity to gather 10 to 15 source-backed links across product launches, benchmarks, and policy updates.

<PromptBlock agent="USER">
Find the most important AI updates this week across tooling, model releases, and developer workflows. Include credible source links.
</PromptBlock>
</WorkflowStep>

<WorkflowStep step={2} title="Cluster and Summarize">
Use ChatGPT to cluster findings into themes and create one-paragraph summaries per theme.

<PromptBlock agent="USER">
Group these updates into 4 themes and summarize each in under 100 words. Include why each theme matters this month.
</PromptBlock>
</WorkflowStep>

<WorkflowStep step={3} title="Draft Publication Copy">
Generate a final digest with intro, theme sections, and a short "what to watch next" ending.
</WorkflowStep>

## Expected Output

A polished digest ready for LUG channels, newsletter, or social posting in under 60 minutes.
