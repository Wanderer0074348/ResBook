## Goal
Turn broad research into a concrete roadmap with milestones and implementation tasks.

<WorkflowStep step={1} title="Source Collection">
Gather high-quality references using <ToolLink slug="perplexity" /> and save them into one brief.
</WorkflowStep>

<WorkflowStep step={2} title="Theme Extraction">
Use <ToolLink slug="notebooklm" /> to cluster sources into themes, assumptions, and unknowns.
</WorkflowStep>

<WorkflowStep step={3} title="Plan Generation">
Use <ToolLink slug="claude-code" /> with model routing from <ToolLink slug="openrouter" /> to generate a phased implementation plan.

<PromptBlock agent="planner">
Generate a goal-backward implementation plan from this research brief. Include milestones, dependencies, risks, and validation criteria.
</PromptBlock>
</WorkflowStep>

## Expected Output
A build-ready plan document that engineering can execute without additional discovery meetings.
