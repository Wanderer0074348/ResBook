## Goal

Learn a new technical topic quickly while maintaining conceptual accuracy and retention.

<WorkflowStep step={1} title="Define Scope and Outcomes">
Pick one topic and define what you should be able to build or explain by day 7.
</WorkflowStep>

<WorkflowStep step={2} title="Daily Learn-Practice-Reflect Loop">
Use ChatGPT/Gemini for explanations, Perplexity for references, and write one daily mini-project or summary.
</WorkflowStep>

<WorkflowStep step={3} title="Final Consolidation">
Generate a revision sheet and 20-question self-test, then identify weak areas for next sprint.

<PromptBlock agent="USER">
Create a revision sheet and 20 assessment questions for this topic. Include answers and common mistakes.
</PromptBlock>
</WorkflowStep>

## Expected Output

A measurable knowledge gain, revision assets, and evidence of practical understanding.
