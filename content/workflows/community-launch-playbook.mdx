## Goal

Launch a new resource site in a community channel with high clarity and strong early engagement.

<WorkflowStep step={1} title="Define Launch Message">
Draft a short value proposition, who it is for, and what members should do first.

<PromptBlock agent="USER">
Write a concise launch message for a markdown-based AI tools and workflows directory. Keep it practical and action-oriented.
</PromptBlock>
</WorkflowStep>

<WorkflowStep step={2} title="Prepare Audience Variants">
Create one version each for student groups, developers, and general users.
</WorkflowStep>

<WorkflowStep step={3} title="Post and Capture Feedback">
Collect comments/questions for 48 hours and tag feedback into content gaps, UX friction, and feature requests.
</WorkflowStep>

## Expected Output

A successful first launch post with clear call-to-action and a structured feedback backlog.
