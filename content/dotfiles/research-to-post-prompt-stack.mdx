## Overview
This stack compresses discovery, synthesis, and drafting into one repeatable flow.

## Prompt Set

<PromptBlock agent="research">
Find 5 current sources on the topic, summarize each in one sentence, and cite the URL.
</PromptBlock>

<PromptBlock agent="synthesizer">
Group findings into 3 themes, identify one contrarian insight, and produce a concise outline.
</PromptBlock>

<PromptBlock agent="writer">
Draft a post in my tone with one hook, three insight bullets, one practical takeaway, and a CTA.
</PromptBlock>

## Tool Pairings
- <ToolLink slug="perplexity" /> for fresh source discovery.
- <ToolLink slug="notebooklm" /> for clustering notes and references.
- <ToolLink slug="chatgpt" /> for final draft refinement.

## Quality Check
Before publishing, verify every claim maps to a source and remove duplicated phrasing.
